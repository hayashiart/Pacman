Rules of Pacman

  • Use arrow keys to move Pacman around the maze.
  • Eat all the yellow dots to advance to the next level.
  • Avoid the ghosts—they’ll take a life if they catch you!
  • Eat a power dot (pink) to turn the tables—ghosts become edible for a short time.
  • Score points: 10 per dot, 50 per power dot, 200 per ghost eaten.
  • You have 3 lives. Lose them all, and it’s game over!
  • Finish all 3 levels to win and earn a time bonus.
